CONTROLS

P - Commence Raider attack
Q - Move down
E - Move up
W - Move forward
S - Move backward
A - Turn left
D - Turn right
Up Arrow - Look up
Down Arrow - Look down

Spacebar - Turns one model wireframe and back (Game Object no. 2, specifically)

Because of the funny starting position, A and D will cause the camera to snap to a specific rotation, but should behave normally after that...
